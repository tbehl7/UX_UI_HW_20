VALUE PROPOSITION

Empowering inventors to start businesss, while saving time and money.

The Problem

The current Inventors Council of Central Florida (ICCF) website is outdated, text heavy, and difficult to navigate.

The Solution

Updating the Inventors Council of Central Florida (ICCF) website navigation, content organization and visual presentation will increase inventor usability, engagement, membership, and retention.

INTERVIEWS & RESEARCH

Key takeaways revealed users had difficulty navigating the site while struggling to finding information.

In retrospect and with more time, we would include more intial user tests with inventors.

IDEATION

Card Sorting
& User Personas

Our team developed a porto-persona as a baseline for ICCF clientele. Followed by user interviews, surveys, and tests with a in-depth heuristics evaluation of the current site. Main takeaways were design, functionality, and usability issues.

VISUAL IDENTITY

Our team was inspired by the innovative solutions within invention process, Technology, Fabrication, and Creativity.

EXECUTION

We transformed the common “lightbulb” metaphor into a transcendent and new experience that ICCF members are excited to share.

RESULT

Client Ecstatic & Overjoyed

Looking ahead on the work that still needs to be done; user tests revealed that our team still needs to iterate on secondary navigation, also (a client request) build for an interactive invention process diagram, there’s a need to generate donation engagement, as well as adding search functionalities.
