About

Attention is fleeting

Attention is fleeting and life is fragile. When I was 18 years old, my world was flipped upside down due to an extreme medical emergency, and I forced to sign consent to operate with a 2% survival rate. To be clear, this 2% meant being a life-long paraplegic. Don’t fret, the outcome was on the contrary, and I’m happy to report that I am 13 years without any limitations. But this experience made me appreciate every aspect of life; and In retrospect, it’s oddly clear as to why I pursued a career in UX. To create incredible experiences that uplift the human condition through digital products.

My experience have shaped my outlook, and my vision for the future…while pushing me to frequently cycle back to my deeply seeded core value; Obsessiveness. The driving desire to self assess and challenge myself to create the best possible work and outcome. This obsessiveness has lead me to roles a product designer, art director, animator, 3D artist, and designer. With over 10 years of experience in the design field, I’ve worked on everything from 3D architectural renderings to brand identity systems, while also creating 3D animations and VFX to designing cross-platform mobile applications using UX methodologies.
